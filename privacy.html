1.Overview

MindMoneyToday ("we", "us", "our") operates a digital content platform and creator toolset focused on the psychology of money. We provide content scheduling, publishing automation, and analytics tools that integrate with social media platforms including TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our platform, tools, and website. By using MindMoneyToday, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2.Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you provide directly:

  • Contact information (email address) when you reach out to us
  • Authorization credentials when you connect third-party platform accounts

Information collected automatically through platform integrations:

Data Type Source Purpose
TikTok account identifier (open_id) TikTok Login Kit Authentication and account linking
TikTok video metadata TikTok Content Posting API Publishing and scheduling content
Video performance metrics TikTok video.list API Analytics and performance reporting
YouTube channel and video data YouTube Data API v3 Publishing and analytics
Platform analytics (views, likes, etc.) Platform APIs Performance reporting and optimization

Information we do not collect:

  • Payment or financial information
  • Sensitive personal data (health, biometric, etc.)
  • Personal data from your followers or audience

3.How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and authorize platform integrations
  • Publish, schedule, and manage content on your behalf to connected platforms
  • Retrieve and display performance analytics for your content
  • Improve the accuracy and reliability of our tools
  • Respond to your inquiries or support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the direct operation of our platform tools.

4.Third-Party Platform Integrations

MindMoneyToday integrates with the following third-party platforms. Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data:

  • TikTok — TikTok Privacy Policy
  • YouTube / Google — Google Privacy Policy
  • Instagram / Meta — Meta Privacy Policy

When you authorize MindMoneyToday to access your account on these platforms, you are also subject to those platforms' terms and privacy policies. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to operate our tools.

You may revoke MindMoneyToday's access to your third-party accounts at any time through each platform's settings.

5.Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  • For platform operations: Transmitting your content to connected platforms on your behalf
  • Legal compliance: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Protection of rights: When necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of MindMoneyToday or others

6.Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encrypted storage of API access tokens and credentials
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Restricted access to stored credentials and platform tokens
  • Regular review of our security practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7.Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law:

  • OAuth access tokens are stored locally and refreshed automatically — they can be deleted at any time
  • Analytics data is fetched on demand and not stored permanently
  • Contact information is retained only while actively needed for support

You may request deletion of any data we hold about you at any time by contacting us.

8.Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your personal data
  • Revocation: Revoke consent or platform access at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at lic.manuelpablos@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9.Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies for marketing or advertising purposes. We do not serve ads, run retargeting campaigns, or share browsing data with advertisers.

Third-party platforms embedded in or linked from our site may use their own cookies in accordance with their privacy policies.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

We take appropriate steps to ensure that transfers of personal data are protected in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
